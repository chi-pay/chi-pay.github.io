Mission at ChiPay.

Make currency exchange and transfers out of China easier for travellers abroad. To offer cryptocurrency exchange services for full financial freedom.

Chipay is closely affiliated with SIMTrip, an international sim card distribution company that serves more than 2 million travellers from the Far East. ChiPay ermeged out of their need for foreign exchange and cryptocurrency. We are looking for partners to provide these solutions.
